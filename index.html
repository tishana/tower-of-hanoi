Towers of Hanoi

by Tishana Trainor

Try to move all of the discs to the last pole.

There's only one rule... You can't place a larger disc atop a smaller disk.

Enjoy!

About Towers of Hanoi...

Towers of Hanoi is a classic puzzle said to be created by French mathematician Edouard Lucas.

According to Wikipedia, the game based on a legend of a Hindu temple where the puzzle is played by young priests.
